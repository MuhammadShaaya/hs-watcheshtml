Our story

A small selection, chosen carefully

H&S Watches began as a simple frustration: most "luxury" watch stores online are either wildly overpriced or full of pieces that look better in a photo than on a wrist. We set out to build the opposite — a short, honest catalogue of watches worth the money, sold with cash-on-delivery so you can inspect before you pay.

What we carry

Automatic, chronograph, diver, GMT and dress watches — sixteen pieces at any time, rotated as new ones are sourced.

How we sell

Cash on delivery, nationwide. You inspect the watch when the courier hands it over, and pay only if you're satisfied.

Who we are

A small team that answers its own inbox. Write to us with a question about any piece before you order — we'd rather you ask.
